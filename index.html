About Me

I am a PhD researcher in Data Sciences & AI at IIT Guwahati (present) with a B.Tech from BPUT Odisha (2024). I convert state-of-the-art research into production: implementing SoTA models (RL, generative models, transformers), building distributed training and inference pipelines, and engineering low-level systems from bootloaders to GPU clusters to complex photorealistic game engines in high level settings like Python. I'm interested in startups to deliver niche, research-driven automation and secure, scalable ML infrastructure.
